2Our Understanding of Requirements
DeepHealthrequires an end-to-end managed localization service that acceleratesglobal healthcare product 
launches  Objective
•DeepHealthrequires a managed, end-to-end localization capability to accelerate global healthcare product launches while meeting clinical,
regulatory, and linguistic standards
•The solution must support localization of complex artifacts such as IFUs, user guides, and training videos that contain mixedcontent (text, 
images, and media) and are not fully supported by standard translation tools
•The solution must enable consistent and coordinated processing of all content types within a single localization workflow, ensuring no loss of 
context or structure
•The solution must enforce mandatory human review and approval for all regulatory-critical content to ensure clinical accuracy, regulatory 
compliance, and language quality
•The solution must deliver fully assembled, validated localized artifacts, preserving original layout, structure, and intent, ready for QA and 
regulatory validation
•The solution must be operated as a managed service, providing the customer with a single point of accountability for quality,timelines, and 
complianceOur Understanding of DeepHealthRequirements

5Assumptions and Dependencies
Product & Scope
Localization is release-critical and planned as part of the product release lifecycle
Initial scope includes UI resource files, IFU/Word documents, and training videos; advanced content types may be added later
Video localization covers audio narration, subtitles, and design-based visuals, not advanced video editing.
Operating Model
The solution is delivered as a CitiusTech-operated managed service
Customers do not self-serve the system; all workflows are initiated and monitored by CitiusTech teams via the web application
Lokaliseacts as the translation and human review backend; CitiusTech owns orchestration, assembly, and delivery
Technology & Integration
Lokaliseis approved by the client for enterprise translation and human review workflows
IFU and video images are assumed to originate from design sources such as Figma, enabling deterministic text extraction and re-rendering without OCR
Video files are accessible in standard formats (e.g., MP4) compatible with Ffmpeg
Speech-to-text and text-to-speech services are available for the required target languages
Review & Compliance
Human review and approval are mandatory for all regulatory-critical artifacts
AI is used only to assist and accelerate review (e.g., completeness, consistency, anomaly detection), not for final approval
Final validation and sign-off remain the responsibility of trained reviewers
Access & Dependencies
Timely access is provided to (Product repositories, Design assets (Figma), Video assets)
Required credentials and approvals (Lokalise, Figma, cloud environments) are available early in the project
Regulatory rules and country-specific requirements remain stable during a release cycle
Deployment Model
The Localization Web Application and backend services will be deployed within the customer’s environment (e.g., customer-managedcloud or approved 
hosting environment)
CitiusTech will deploy, configure, and operate the solution as a managed service, while respecting the customer’s security, compliance, and IT policies
